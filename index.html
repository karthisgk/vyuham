About Us

The MCA Department has started Students Association 'EITA'-Emerging Information Technologist Association in the year 2002. This association was started with a vision of motivating students in par with current Trends and Technologies of the IT industry. Every year we organize State Level Technical Symposium - VYUHAM for the students to enhance the multi skills . The Symposium has organized to foster technical competence among students in their domain. The VYUHAM'19 will be a open platform for the students to present their innovative ideas and develop technical skills of Heterogeneous participants from various academic Institutions. We delightfully welcome you all to examine yourself in different areas on September 27th 2019.

CLASH OF BRAINS (HUNTING)

RULES:
  • The maximum number of participants per team should be two.
  • Any number of teams per college can participate in this event.
  • The decision of the judges will be final.
  • The rules are subject to change.
ROUND-1:
  • Time - Duration: 25 minutes.
  • Participants should guess technical words based on given images as clues.
  • Number of questions will be 25 and each question must be answered within 1 minute.
  • Top scorers of this round will be qualified for “Round-2”.
ROUND-2:
  • Time - Duration: 25 minutes.
  • Participants should guess technical words based on given images as clues.
  • Number of questions will be 25 and each question must be answered within 1 minute.
  • Top scorers of this round will be the winners.
  • If there is tie, it will be resolved in a tie breaker.
